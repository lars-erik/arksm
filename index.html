To start your LTKSM experience, make sure the button at the bottom does not say AR NOT SUPPORTED.
If it does, you're out of luck. Try a mobile device such as an Android phone.
Otherwise, go ahead and click it to bring it on.

When you see your camera input, slowly move the camera around to let it map out the area. If you do it right, Little Travelling KSM will appear out of thin air.

This might just crash with an "Aw snap" as well. Then it might work to open the page in an incognito tab.
